SymphoCubes! The symphogears but now in printable cube form!

Originally written December 18th, 2022.

"Because I was bored", as Leo Bodnar said when he did the Headless eMac writeup many years ago.

All images are from official source material or from the vast collection of Symphogear reaction images (Requires HTTPS). I stole the template from google images but their URL is still present. :^)

All you need is a printer, a pair of scissors, and some school glue (can be a bottle or a gluestick). Double sided tape also works.

My cat liked the Maria one.
